# Product Vision & Repositioning

## New positioning

### Before
- Family photo album / journal
- Highly competitive category with many substitutes

### After
**AI-powered achievement portfolio and long-term guidance platform for kids**

Core idea:
- Collect structured “achievement data” over time (projects, activities, awards, reflections).
- Use AI to extract strengths, patterns, and growth narratives.
- Generate private, shareable outputs for specific contexts (e.g., school applications), not public social sharing.

## Naming directions

Shortlist:
- **AchieveTrack**: emphasizes longitudinal achievement tracking
- **FuturePortfolio**: emphasizes outcomes (portfolio)
- **TalentSeed**: emphasizes discovery of emerging strengths

## Elevator pitch (30 seconds)

“AchieveTrack helps families capture a child’s achievements over 5–10+ years and uses AI
to identify strengths, recommend next steps (clubs, projects, courses), and generate
portfolio materials for school applications. It is private by default and optimized for
structured evidence—not social posting.”

## Value proposition

Problem:
- Achievement evidence is scattered (photos, certificates, notes, chats).
- Creating portfolios for applications is time-consuming and inconsistent.
- Families lack an objective, data-driven way to see strengths over time.

Solution:
- Centralize achievements with structured metadata.
- AI summarizes patterns and strengths and suggests next steps.
- Portfolio generator exports curated narratives and artifacts (PDF/web).

Differentiation:
- Versus photo sharing apps: analysis and portfolio generation.
- Versus education content platforms: longitudinal evidence + storytelling.
- Versus portfolio tools: automated extraction from raw life data + AI guidance.
